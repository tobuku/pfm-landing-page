The Problem Every Remote Investor Faces

You Can't Be at Every Property.
But You Need to Know What's There.

When you own property in another city, verifying its condition requires time, money, and travel you don't always have. Before requesting a loan draw or making investment decisions, you need fast, independent visual proof.

Travel is expensive

Flights, hotels, and lost time add up — especially across multiple out-of-state properties.

Inspectors take days

Traditional inspection services are slow, expensive, and overkill when you just need current photos and video.

Draws get delayed

Hard money lenders require proof before releasing funds. Without documentation, your rehab timeline stalls.
